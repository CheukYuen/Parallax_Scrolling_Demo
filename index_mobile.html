What is Picture the Possibilities?

PTP Youth learn the 7 Powers of Creating program that teaches them how to create anything from art to science.They practice this power by making films (with mentors) on content vital to global youth. Seeing how to bring a picture from nothing into reality, they can then apply this creative process to other areas of their lives and dreams. PTP also creates personal profiles on each participant sharing their stories and dreams with the world.

Picture the Possibilities Summit

The annual Picture the Possibilities Summit is part of CREATICS. PTP youth, from around the world, converge in Silicon Valley to connect with leaders, company employees and global luminaries to share their creations and empower each other. Ultimately, PTP empowers global youth with the creativity and innovations to improve worlds and create their dreams.
